Most agencies hand you a deck. We hand you a render you can show your CEO. The first call is
60 minutes of structured questions about your audience, the show, and the business outcome
you're chasing. The second deliverable, usually four days later, is a 3D render of your booth
sitting in the actual hall — same floor, same lighting, same neighbour booths.

Iteration happens in real-time over a shared model, not over a slow review cycle. Most clients
sign off inside seven calendar days. Then production starts the same week.
